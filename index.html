OUR SPECIALIZATION

Who Are We, Really?

Fan Bhangre De was founded in 2021 by Bhupinder Singh, a passionate performer with a vision to promote authentic Punjabi folk dance in the modern world. What began as a small dance group has grown into a thriving community of learners and performers. Over the past three years, we’ve trained students of all ages, performed at major live events, and provided choreography and dancers for weddings, cultural shows, and live singers. With a focus on authenticity, energy, and excellence, we’re proud to share the vibrant spirit of Punjab with every beat.

Meet the Instructor

Why Join Fan Bhangre De?

Fan Bhangre De is more than a dance school — it’s a vibrant community rooted deeply in Punjabi culture and Sikh traditions. Our classes celebrate not only the energetic art of Bhangra and Giddha but also the rich heritage, values, and spirituality that unite us. Through dance, we honor our history, strengthen community bonds, and pass down meaningful customs to every generation. Join us to be part of a supportive family that embraces culture, faith, and togetherness with every step.

How Do You Join Our Cultural Family?

Joining Fan Bhangre De means stepping into a warm, welcoming community that cherishes Punjabi culture, faith, and tradition. Here, dance is more than movement — it’s a celebration of identity and togetherness. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced performer, you’ll find support, friendship, and a shared passion that connects generations. Through classes, events, and performances, we nurture not just skills but lifelong bonds rooted in respect, heritage, and joy. Become part of our family and keep the spirit of Punjab alive in every beat.
